Test QR Code Generator

Generate test QR codes to scan with your attendance system.

How to use:

  1. Enter a test QR token (e.g., "test-qr-123")
  2. Click "Generate QR Code"
  3. Use your phone/tablet to scan this QR code with the attendance system

Test QR Tokens:

  • test-qr-123 - Basic test token
  • daily-qr-2024-01-15 - Daily QR format
  • attendance-test - Simple test token
